How We Use Your Data

Specifically, we use your data to:

Identify and authenticate your account using your phone number via OTP. Display your profile to other users of the platform. Allow clients to discover, contact, and chat with experts. Data Storage & Retention

All user data is stored on our own PocketBase server hosted at clickoy.in, located in India. We do not use third-party cloud databases such as Firebase, AWS, or Azure for storing personal data.

Retention: Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account through the app, all your personal data, chat history, documents, and records are permanently deleted from our servers with no recovery possible.

Uploaded files (profile photos, documents, portfolio images) are stored as files on the same server and deleted alongside your account record upon account deletion.

Your Rights

You have the following rights over your personal data at all times:

✅ Access your data ✅ Correct your data ✅ Delete your account ✅ Export your data ✅ Withdraw consent

Delete account: Open the Clickoy app → Profile → scroll to the bottom → tap Delete Account. This immediately and permanently deletes all your data from our servers. You can also email us at care [at] clickoy.in to request deletion.

Edit your data: You can update your name, photo, email, and other profile details at any time from within the app.

Data export / access requests: Email us at care [at] clickoy.in and we will respond within 30 days as required under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India).

7

Children's Privacy

Clickoy is not intended for use by persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. Expert registration requires date of birth verification to confirm the user is 18 or older.

If you believe a minor has created an account, please contact us at care [at] clickoy.in and we will delete the account immediately.

8

Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS (TLS). Our server enforces cleartext: false — plain HTTP connections are rejected.

Account access is protected by OTP-based login — there are no passwords to leak. Sensitive documents (Aadhaar, DL) are stored as private files accessible only through authenticated requests.

While we take reasonable measures to protect your data, no system is 100% secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it responsibly to care [at] clickoy.in.
